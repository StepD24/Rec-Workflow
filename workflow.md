# Recruitment Workflow Diagram

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[Pre-Recruitment Activities] --> B[Finalize ToA]
    B --> C[Publish Vacancy]
    C --> D[Post Vacancy Notice]
    D --> E[Period of Publication]
    E --> F[Check Status]
    F --> G[Strategize Outreach]
    G --> H[Source Candidates]
    H --> I[Monitor Status]
    I --> J[Circular to NCBs]
    J --> K[Support Letters from NCBs]
    K --> L[Manage Candidate Pool]
    L --> M[Longlisting]
    M --> N[Shortlisting]
    N --> O[Shortlist Validation]
    O --> P[Written Assessment]
    P --> Q[Grading of Tests]
    Q --> R[Conducting Interviews]
    R --> S[Prepare for Interview]
    S --> T[Day of Interview]
    T --> U[During Each Interview Session]
    U --> V[Post-Interview Activities]
    V --> W[Collect Feedback]
    W --> X[Assessment Report/Candidate Selection]
    X --> Y[Approval Process]
    Y --> Z[Notify Candidates]
    Z --> AA[Manage Job Offer (Contracted Staff)]
    AA --> AB[Collecting Information and Background Check]
    AB --> AC[Acceptance of Offer]
    AC --> AD[Manage Selection Letter (Secondments)]
    AD --> AE[Selection Letter]
    AE --> AF[NCB Confirmation]
    AF --> AG[Finalizing Onboarding]
    AG --> AH[Official Start Date]
